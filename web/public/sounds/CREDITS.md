## Binaural 40Hz
- **Source**: Self-synthesized — generated live in the browser via the Web Audio API: a 200Hz sine tone in the left channel and a 240Hz sine tone in the right channel, producing a 40Hz binaural beat (see `binauralBeat.js`).
